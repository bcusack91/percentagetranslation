Translating Percents

Welcome to the exciting world of SAT percentages! In order to understand percentages, we'll first explain how to interpret percentage word problems and create mathematical equations.

The following list is a key to help you convert percentage word problems into mathematical equations. The word(s) on the left translate to the symbol on the right:

  • is or has \(\Rightarrow \) =
  • of \(\Rightarrow \cdot\) (multiplication symbol)
  • percent \(\Rightarrow \frac{}{100}\)
  • What percent \( \Rightarrow \frac{x}{100} \)
  • What Number \( \Rightarrow x, y, n\) (any variable)
  • Some Number \( \Rightarrow x, y, n\) (any variable)
